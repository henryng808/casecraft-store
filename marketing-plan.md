# CaseCraft AI: Global Cross-Border E-Commerce & X (Twitter) Marketing Strategy

This plan was developed by the **Cross-Border E-Commerce Specialist** and **Twitter Engager** Agency Agents to fast-track your phone case business into the global market.

---

## 🏗️ 1. Store Operations (The Foundation)
**Agent: Cross-Border E-Commerce Specialist**

### Platform Choice: Shopify (DTC Independent Site)
*   **Why**: Full control over brand identity, data, and direct integration with X (Twitter) Ads/Pixel.
*   **Setup Focus**: 
    *   **Trust Signals**: Include clear "Free International Shipping" and "30-Day Money Back Guarantee" badges.
    *   **Mobile-First**: 85% of Twitter users are on mobile. The site MUST be ultra-fast.
    *   **Currency**: Use a multi-currency switcher (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY).

### Logistics & Fulfillment
*   **Initial Phase**: Use **Dropshipping** via CJ Dropshipping or AliExpress to test the market without inventory risk.
*   **Scaling Phase**: Once a SKU sells 20+ units/day, move to an **Overseas Warehouse (USA/EU)** for 3-5 day delivery.

### Compliance
*   **VAT/Tax**: Register for IOSS (EU) to handle taxes at checkout for a smoother customer experience.
*   **HS Code**: Use `4202.32` for plastic/leather phone cases to avoid customs delays.

## 🐦 2. X (Twitter) Marketing Strategy
**Agent: Twitter Engager**

### Content Mix (The 4-2-2-2 Rule)
1.  **40% Educational/Value**: "How to protect your phone screen from micro-scratches" or "Top 5 Tech accessories for minimalist desks."
2.  **20% Behind the Scenes**: AI-generated design process, stress-testing videos (using your Video Factory).
3.  **20% Community Engagement**: Polls like "Which color for the next iPhone 16 drop? [Midnight / Cobalt / Forest]"
4.  **20% Hard Sell**: "Final 24 hours for 15% off. Link in bio."

### Viral Hook Formulas
*   **The "Anti-Bulk" Hook**: "Stop carrying a brick. 🧱 Our 0.3mm CaseCraft series feels like nothing, but protects like everything."
*   **The "MagSafe" Hook**: "Why is your charger always falling off? ⚡ Most cases block the magnets. Ours reinforces them."

### Daily Workflow
*   **Morning**: Reply to 10 top tech influencers (e.g., @MKBHD, @UnboxTherapy) with value-add comments (not spam).
*   **Noon**: Post 1 high-quality visual (AI-generated product photo).
*   **Evening**: RT a customer review or tag a "setup of the day" that uses your case.
